About Us

QR Med is built to give patients control over their health data. Instead of carrying bulky files or worrying about privacy, patients can now upload medical reports securely and generate a unique QR code. This QR code can be scanned by doctors to instantly access reports in a safe, encrypted manner — ensuring efficiency, transparency, and peace of mind.


Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize healthcare data sharing by making it secure, patient-centric, and effortless. We believe that health data belongs to patients — and they should decide how and when it is shared. With QR Med, we aim to eliminate inefficiencies in healthcare systems, prevent data misuse, and empower patients to take control of their medical history.

What We Do

With QR Med, patients can:

  • Upload medical reports securely (PDFs only)
  • Generate unique QR codes instantly
  • Allow doctors to scan and access reports securely
  • View access logs to track who viewed their reports

Who We Help

  • Patients: Securely manage and share health records.
  • Doctors: Access patient reports instantly via QR codes.
  • Hospitals & Clinics: Reduce paperwork and streamline data sharing.
  • Healthcare Systems: Improve efficiency and security in record management.

Our Vision

We envision a healthcare system where patients truly own their data, doctors get quick access to critical information, and healthcare delivery becomes faster and more secure. QR Med is a step toward a future where technology ensures health data remains private, portable, and patient-first.
